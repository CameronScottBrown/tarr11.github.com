LinkedIn Social Connector Doesn't Work With 64 Bit Office

Aug 27th, 2010

If you have a 64 bit version of Microsoft Office, you will not be able to use LinkedIn's social connector.  It claims to install, but then you do not see any new social connectors installed.  

So, if you see that your social connector list is blank, or you can't find your LinkedIn social connector, or if your linkedIn social connector is not working, it may be because you are on 64 bit windows.

According to this link, LinkedIn is currently working on a 64-bit social connector. 

In the meantime, you can try Facebook's 64 bit social connector.
